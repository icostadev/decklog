# Decision: Apple Pay over Stripe Link

**Context.** We want a one-tap payment method for the beta.

**Options.** Apple Pay; Stripe Link.

**Decision.** Apple Pay first — highest share of our mobile traffic is on iOS, and it
needs no extra account step. Stripe Link is a fast follow.
