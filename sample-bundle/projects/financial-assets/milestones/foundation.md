The first usable version: model assets (market-priced and manually-valued),
record holdings and valuations in their native currency, convert to a base
currency, and see total net worth broken down by region and asset type.

No external data feeds yet — prices and FX rates are entered manually so the
core tracker is useful and testable on its own before the integration work in
[Market data](/projects/financial-assets/milestones/market-data.md).
